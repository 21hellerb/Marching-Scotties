Band Boosters

About the Band Boosters

The band boosters assist the band director with competition fees, uniforms, band t-shirts, and organizing the end of the year party.

2018/19 Executive Board

  1. President: Robert Scranton
  2. Vice President: Dawn Schiariti
  3. Secretary: Heather Comisky
  4. Corresponding Secretary: Denise Demkowicz
  5. Treasurer: Inge Pratt

You can meet with the executive board after the two practice days (May 30 and June 6) if you are a new parent or a returning one with questions.


Note: With the band growing, we need to raise the marching band fee to $50 per students, and if there are siblings, the second child will cost $35. Also, please write out a check to the Booster parents, NO cash please.
